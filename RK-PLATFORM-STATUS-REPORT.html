5. Next Steps — The Idea Pipeline

How the Idea Pipeline Works

The Idea Pipeline is a voice-first workflow designed for CEO Joe Maclean and leadership to capture, optimize, and deploy ideas directly into the platform. Here is how it works:

Step-by-Step Workflow

1Speak Into the Mic

Navigate to the IDEA section in the sidebar (/#/voice-capture). Click the mic button and speak your idea naturally. When speaking, include the following:

  • Department Name — Which department this idea applies to (e.g., "This is for the Operations team" or "This is a Sales & BD feature")
  • Feature Description — What you want added and what it should do (e.g., "I want a contract escalation alert that shows me every lease renewal within 60 days")
  • Data Source — Where the data is coming from, if applicable (e.g., "The data comes from our Insightly CRM" or "Pull this from the TMS feed" or "This is from our QuickBooks AR aging report")

2AI Optimizes Your Notes

Once you stop recording, the platform automatically:

  • Transcribes your voice using OpenAI Whisper (with RK-specific terminology prompting)
  • Structures the idea using GPT-4o — generates a clear title, detailed description, category, estimated business impact, and 3-5 actionable next steps
  • You can review and edit any field the AI generated before approving

3Add CEO Notes & Approve

Add any additional context, priority notes, or specific instructions in the CEO Notes field. Then choose:

  • "Approve & Send to Dev" — The idea is immediately visible in the Dev Bucket
  • "Save as Draft" — Saves for later review without sending to the dev team
  • "Discard" — Clears the idea entirely

4Dev Team Receives the Idea

Approved ideas appear as cards in the Dev Bucket (/#/dev-bucket). Each card shows the title, description, category, impact estimate, AI suggestions, and CEO notes. The dev team can:

  • Start Work — Move status from "Approved" to "In Progress"
  • Add Dev Notes — Write updates, questions, or technical notes back to Joe
  • Mark Complete — Signal that the feature has been built and deployed
  • Send Back — Return the idea with notes if it needs refinement or isn't feasible

5Joe Sees Status Updates

In the Voice to Idea page, Joe's recent ideas panel shows real-time status for every idea he has submitted. When the dev team adds notes or changes status, it is immediately visible. Ideas marked "Completed" confirm the feature is live.

Example Voice Input

"This is for the Operations department. I want a feature on the Facilities page that shows every commercial real estate contract per building, with the customer name, the lease start and end date, the rate, and any escalation clauses. When an escalation date is within 30 days, it should flag it red. The data is coming from our shared drive lease files and Susanna's tracking spreadsheets. This should free up Susanna and Jasmine from manually tracking this stuff every week."

This voice input would be transcribed, optimized into a structured feature spec with title, description, category ("Operations"), impact estimate, and next steps — then sent to the dev team for implementation.
